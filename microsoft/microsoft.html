Color Codes and Scores Explanation:

  • Green: Low severity (Base Score < 4).
  • Yellow: Medium severity (Base Score 4-6.9).
  • Red: High severity (Base Score ≥ 7).
  • Gray: Score unavailable (N/A).

Base Score: Represents the severity of the vulnerability on its own.
Temporal Score: Adjusts the severity based on factors like exploitability and remediation.
